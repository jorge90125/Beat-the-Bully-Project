Premise

The school bully won't stop picking on you and your friends! After speaking out against him, he's challenging you to a fight after school. You only have a limited amount of time to train up! First thing's first, what is your name?

Directions

  • Add points to your 3 skills
  • The sum total of all 3 skills must equal 10 to continue
  • Click the "Start Batlle" button when you are done and the battle will commence
  • Once the battle begins, each turn will consist of one of the 3 skills being randomly chosen
  • The random skill value will be compared to a random skill value of the bully
  • The player with the higher stat will deal one damage
  • Once a player's health reaches 0, they lose
